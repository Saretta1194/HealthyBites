Berry Protein Smoothie Bowl

Ingredients:

  • Banana
  • Mixed berries
  • Greek yogurt
  • Protein powder (optional)
  • Granola

Instructions:

  • Blend banana, berries, yogurt, and protein powder.
  • Pour into a bowl, top with granola, and enjoy!

Overnight Oats with Chia & Honey

Ingredients:

  • Rolled oats
  • Chia seeds
  • Almond milk
  • Honey
  • Banana

Instructions:

  • Mix oats, chia seeds, and milk in a jar.
  • Refrigerate overnight, then add banana and honey before eating.

Quinoa & Chickpea Salad

Ingredients:

  • Cooked quinoa
  • Chickpeas
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Avocado
  • Lemon juice

Instructions:

  • Mix all ingredients in a bowl.
  • Drizzle with lemon juice, toss well, and serve.

Grilled Chicken & Hummus Wrap

Ingredients:

  • Whole-wheat tortilla
  • Grilled chicken
  • Hummus
  • Spinach
  • Cucumber

Instructions:

  • Spread hummus on tortilla, add ingredients.
  • Roll tightly, slice, and serve.

Peanut Butter Energy Balls

Ingredients:

  • Oats
  • Peanut butter
  • Honey
  • Chia seeds

Instructions:

  • Mix all ingredients, roll into balls.
  • Refrigerate for 20 minutes and enjoy.
